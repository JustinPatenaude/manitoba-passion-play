Experience the greatest story ever told.

2018 marks our 19th Season!

Once again Oak Valley Productions Inc. presents the inspiring drama of Manitoba's Passion Play at its scenic outdoor theatre site near La Riviere, Manitoba. Numerous volunteers from all across southern Manitoba, including cast, crew, front of house personnel and many more work together to present this unique Manitoba event. Year after year many are still discovering this hidden gem.

Live Orchestra

Returning for 2018, a group of musicians led by conductor Jason Vanwynsberghe of Pilot Mound come from across Southern Manitoba and have been rehearsing and volunteering their time and talents to provide musical accompaniment composed by Micheal Janzen and commissioned by Oak Valley for Manitoba's Passion Play.
